What is AllerGPS?

AllerGPS is a dynamic web application built for those who have allergies that hinder them from eating certain foods.

It takes into account these allergies to chart a map of locations that the user can go to and enjoy without worrying about their allergies.

AllerGPS was made to be simple, elegant, and powerful.
